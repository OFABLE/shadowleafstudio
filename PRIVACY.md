# Shadowleaf Studio — Privacy Policy
Last updated: 2026-04-18
## What this applies to
This policy covers the Shadowleaf Studio Pinterest automation app,
which drafts and posts pins promoting Gumroad affiliate products on
behalf of the Shadowleaf Studio Pinterest account holder. It is not a
multi-user service — the only Pinterest user the app ever acts on
behalf of is the account owner who authorized it.
## Data the app accesses via the Pinterest API
With the scopes granted during OAuth authorization, the app may read:
- the owner's Pinterest account profile (username, account type,
  follower count, board count)
- the owner's list of boards
- the owner's existing pins and their public engagement metrics
With the same scopes the app may write:
- new pins on the owner's boards
- edits to pins the owner already created
The app does NOT access, collect, or store any data about any other
Pinterest user, including followers, the people who engage with pins,
or audience-wide analytics.
## Data storage
Pinterest OAuth tokens (the app's authorization to act as the account
owner) are stored only on the account owner's personal computer in a
single local JSON file that is not transmitted anywhere else. Pin
drafts are stored as Markdown files on the same computer.
The app does not operate any server, database, or cloud-hosted
component.
## Data sharing
No data accessed through the Pinterest API is shared with third
parties, sold, or used for advertising outside of Pinterest. Links
published in pin descriptions point to Gumroad product pages that the
owner has chosen to promote; those Gumroad pages are subject to
Gumroad's separate privacy policy at https://gumroad.com/privacy.
## Retention and deletion
The owner can revoke the app's Pinterest access at any time from
https://www.pinterest.com/settings/apps, which immediately invalidates
the stored OAuth tokens. Deleting the local configuration file removes
the tokens from the owner's computer.
## Contact
Questions about this policy or the app's behavior: use the contact
email registered with the Pinterest developer account.
